Lions

Origin

Lions originated in Africa. That is what genetic studies suggest.Eastern and Southern Africa to be exact. one thing that most people do not know, is that there is sub species of lions.

    Barbary Lions(North Africa)
    Cave Lions(Europe)
    The American Lion(North and Central America
    Asiatic Lion(Asia)

Food

Lions are carnivores. This means that they love to eat meat. Lions are apex predators. they love to hunt their food, meaning stalk and hunting prey to eat like:

    Antelopes
    Buffalo
    Zebras
    Young Elephants
    Rhinos
    Hippos
    Wart hogs
    Crocodiles
    Girraffes
